Ground Improvement Using Deep Cement Mixing

In AGSR lab, deep cement mixing (DCM) is one of the effective techniques focussed for ground improvement of soft soils along with other conventional techniques like using chemical and biological additives. Physical modelling and numerical modelling are developed to long-term stress-strain behaviour, strength gain, settlement behaviour and many more.
